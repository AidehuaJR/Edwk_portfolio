About me
Designing is my passion

It was the journey, not the result, one of the most meaningful achievement was I’ve improving UI design at UCSC. It wasn’t just like playing a game, it was about creating them. I loved the entire process: planning, designing, getting user feedback, and seeing it all come together. That creative flow reminded me why I enjoy game development. It’s not just about visuals or code, but it’s about crafting interactive, meaningful experiences with limited tools.
